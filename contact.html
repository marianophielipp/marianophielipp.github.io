Research Interests

My current research focuses on advancing humanoid robotics through Visual Language Action (VLA) models, Human-Robot Interaction (HRI) projects, AI for science applications, and deep reinforcement learning. I'm particularly interested in developing systems that can integrate vision, language, and action to create more capable and intuitive robotic systems.

I'm always open to discussing new research directions and potential collaborations that align with these interests.
