Publications

Research Contributions in AI, Robotics, and Machine Learning

January 1, 2025 Mariano Phielipp

AI for Science

Papers in Digital Discovery and NeurIPS with MILA and the University of Toronto. This work focuses on applying artificial intelligence to accelerate scientific discovery and innovation across various domains including materials science, chemistry, and other scientific fields.

AI for Electronic Design Automation (EDA)

Contributions to ICCAD 2024 and IEEE-ITC 2024. This research focuses on applying artificial intelligence to improve electronic design automation processes, including solving constrained combinatorial optimization problems in chip design and achieving significant improvements in floorplanning techniques.

Robot Learning

Research on visual sparse-reward learning, model-based adversarial imitation learning, and modular language-conditioned robot policies. This work advances the field of robot learning by developing systems that can learn complex tasks through various modalities and improve human-robot interaction.

Foundational Model for Circuit Modeling & Control

Research on pretraining graph neural networks and learning transferable policies. This work focuses on creating base models that can be adapted to various tasks and domains, particularly in the context of circuit modeling and control systems.

Deep Reinforcement Learning

Techniques such as Determinantal Point Process-based neural network sampling. This research advances the field of deep reinforcement learning by developing novel algorithms and techniques for improving learning efficiency and performance.
