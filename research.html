Robot Learning & Reinforcement Learning

Advancing autonomous systems through intelligent learning

October 1, 2024 Mariano Phielipp

My research in robot learning focuses on developing efficient and scalable reinforcement learning systems that enable robots to learn complex tasks from limited data. This includes work on offline-to-online learning, multi-task learning, and human-robot interaction.

Key Research Areas

  • Offline-to-Online Learning: Developing methods to pre-train robots on offline data and fine-tune them for online deployment
  • Multi-Task Learning: Creating systems that can learn multiple related tasks efficiently
  • Human-Robot Interaction: Enabling robots to understand and respond to human instructions and demonstrations
  • Language-Conditioned Policies: Building robots that can follow natural language instructions

Recent Publications

  • "Moto: Offline pre-training to online fine-tuning for model-based robot learning" (CoRL 2023)
  • "Learning modular language-conditioned robot policies through attention" (Autonomous Robots 2023)
  • "Robot learning from human demonstrations: A survey" (Robotics and Autonomous Systems)

AI for Materials Science

Accelerating materials discovery through machine learning

September 15, 2024 Mariano Phielipp

My work in AI for materials science focuses on developing machine learning methods to accelerate the discovery and optimization of new materials. This includes crystal design, molecular discovery, and materials property prediction.

Research Focus

  • Crystal Design: Using reinforcement learning to design novel crystal structures with desired properties
  • Molecular Discovery: Developing methods to discover new molecules for specific applications
  • Materials Optimization: Optimizing material properties through machine learning
  • Property Prediction: Predicting material properties from structure and composition

AI for Electronic Design Automation (EDA)

Optimizing chip design through artificial intelligence

July 10, 2024 Mariano Phielipp

My work in AI for EDA focuses on developing machine learning methods to optimize chip design processes. This includes floorplanning, routing, timing analysis, and power optimization.

Research Focus

  • Floorplanning: Using AI to optimize the placement of circuit blocks on a chip

Neural Networks & Optimization

Advancing deep learning architectures and optimization techniques

July 15, 2024 Mariano Phielipp

My research in neural networks and optimization focuses on developing more efficient, robust, and scalable deep learning systems. This includes work on neural network architecture design, optimization algorithms, and techniques for improving the performance and reliability of AI systems.

We explore various approaches including neuroevolution, multi-objective optimization, ensemble methods, and techniques for handling uncertainty in neural networks. This research has applications across multiple domains including computer vision, natural language processing, and robotics.

Key Publications:

  • "Neuroevolution-enhanced multi-objective optimization for mixed-precision quantization" (GECCO 2022)
  • "DNS: Determinantal point process based neural network sampler for ensemble reinforcement learning" (ICML 2022)
  • "Learning intrinsic symbolic rewards in reinforcement learning" (IJCNN 2022)

Research Topics:

  • Neural Architecture Search
  • Multi-Objective Optimization
  • Ensemble Learning Methods
  • Mixed-Precision Quantization
  • Symbolic Reward Learning
  • Uncertainty Quantification
