About Mariano Phielipp

Principal Engineer, Humanoid | PhD in Computer Science

January 1, 2025 Mariano Phielipp

Professional Overview

I am a Principal Engineer at Humanoid, where I lead the Visual Language Action development for the company. This role focuses on advancing applied research and development in humanoid robotics, integrating AI-driven solutions to enhance robotic functionalities and improve human-robot interactions.

With 18+ years of experience in AI and machine learning, I am highly proficient in Reinforcement Learning, Machine Learning, and Artificial Intelligence. I am known for leading initiatives in robotic learning, computer vision, face recognition and detection, object categorization, and recommendation systems. I am recognized for pioneering solutions in online learning, automatic rule learning, natural language processing, knowledge representation, and energy-based algorithms.

Intel Corporation Experience (2006-2024)

Principal Engineer, Intel AI Labs (April 2020 – August 2024)

Senior Deep Learning Data Scientist (September 2017 – 2020)
